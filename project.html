Stardew Valley

About

Stardew Valley is a game about farming, community and living off the land. You become the owner of your grandfather's farm after you grow fed up with life in the city. You meet a colorful cast of creatures and characters, help the town and rebuild your grandfather's farm to its former glory.

Why its Cool!

If you don't like farming their is still a plethora of things to do! You can go fishing, fight monsters in an abandoned mine to find forgotten treasures, solve puzzles, talk to and romance the towns folk, enjoy seasonal festivals and visit exotic islands! There is something for everyone!
